Our Programs

Everything We Do for Children

Seven programs, one goal: helping children heal, build empathy, and carry peace-building skills with them for life.

Program 01

Peace Clubs

Regular after-school clubs featuring interactive storytelling, peace-themed arts and crafts, puppet shows, and collaborative team-building games. Peace Clubs give children a safe, joyful space to practice empathy and cooperation outside the classroom.

Program 02

Trauma Healing & Psycho-Social Support

Structured psychological rehabilitation, therapeutic play, emotional recovery exercises, and safe counseling spaces for children impacted by societal conflicts and community misfortunes. This is the heart of our healing work.

Program 03

Immersive Summer Camps

Annual specialized camps focused on cross-cultural friendship, outdoor leadership, peer-mediation training, and cooperative sports — giving children a joyful, hands-on space to build lifelong peace-building habits together.

Program 04

Peer Mediation Training

Equipping older children (ages 8–11) with basic negotiation, active listening, and playground dispute resolution skills — so peace-building becomes something children lead themselves, not just something taught to them.

Program 05

School Curriculums & Classroom Rules

Designing and integrating age-appropriate peace education toolkits, daily emotional check-in charts, and non-punitive "peace corner" systems directly into primary schools.

Program 06

Parent & Community Partnerships

Hosting regular interactive sessions with Parent-Teacher Associations (PTAs), community groups, and women's meetings to unify peace-building and trauma-healing vocabulary between the classroom and the home.

Program 07

Peace Parades & Campaigns

Organizing child-led community awareness walks, community peace murals, and public storytelling events that carry the message of non-violence beyond the school gates and into the wider community.
