Teacher Training

Equipping Teachers to Lead Peace in the Classroom

Classroom teachers are the frontline facilitators of early childhood development. Our training model builds their capacity across three core areas.

01

Pedagogical Peace Frameworks

We equip nursery and primary school educators with specialized methodologies to seamlessly integrate conflict-resolution language directly into everyday teaching, arts, and play activities — so peace education isn't a separate subject, but part of how every lesson is taught.

02

Restorative Classroom Management

Teachers move away from punitive discipline strategies and instead establish active, school-based "peace corners" where children can self-regulate and safely navigate peer disputes — replacing punishment with problem-solving.

03

Trauma-Informed Rehabilitation & Intervention

Using educational psychology principles, teachers learn to spot early indicators of trauma, anxiety, or behavioral aggression stemming from societal conflicts and misfortune. They're trained in proactive emotional regulation, grounding exercises, and healing-centered care — before negative behavioral cycles solidify.
