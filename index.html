Our Mission

Healing the wounds of conflict, one classroom at a time.

We provide psycho-social rehabilitation for children affected by societal conflict and misfortune, while training teachers to build peace corners, spot early trauma, and replace punishment with restorative care — embedding empathy into every school day.
