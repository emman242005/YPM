Why It Matters

Every Contribution Reaches a Real Child

As a registered non-profit under Cameroon Law No. 90/053, every franc given to Young Peacemakers Organization is strictly non-distributable and used only to achieve our mission. Bank withdrawals require joint signatures from the President and Treasurer, ensuring your support is handled with full accountability.
