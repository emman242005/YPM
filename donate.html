Where Your Donation Goes

As a registered non-profit under Cameroon Law No. 90/053, every donation is strictly non-distributable and used only to fund trauma healing support, teacher training, peace clubs, and summer camps. Bank withdrawals require joint signatures from the President and Treasurer.
